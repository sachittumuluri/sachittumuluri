### hey, I'm Sachit

CS, data science, and statistics at UMass Amherst (Commonwealth Honors). I build software that listens — to markets, to heartbeats, to music. Not a metaphor: those are literally the three things in this account.

By day I'm a senior analyst at **MAIF** — the fund's only sophomore on the senior desk — where I led the build of our standardized backtesting framework and shipped a multi-agent RL system for VIX futures. I also run engineering on **SmartChart**, a provisionally patented computer-vision app that does EMT paperwork so EMTs don't have to: 350 users across 37 cities and counting. Nights go to **Vega Labs** — AI agents for sales pipelines. The philosophy fits on a tape label: *judgment in the model, rules in code, receipts for everything.*

The heartbeats thing? Published in IEEE proceedings for detecting ventricular tachycardia with algebraic topology. Donut math, 75 patients, peer-reviewed.

### the rack

**// quant desk** — one engine, two cuts

- **[strattrust](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/strattrust)** — a local-first validation layer for trading strategies: same data, same execution assumptions, reproducible scorecards another analyst can re-run. If it can't survive a re-run, it wasn't alpha. `python · docker`
- **[maif-backtester](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/maif-backtester)** — side B of the same tape: the MAIF-branded framework and hosted terminal it grew out of. Event-driven sim, OCO brackets, Monte Carlo stress tests, GAN-generated market regimes. `python · jupyter`

**// research wing**

- **[VentriCode](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/VentriCode)** — ventricular tachycardia detection via Takens embeddings and persistent homology. Cheaper arrhythmia screening, IEEE-published. The [paper repo](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/Applications-of-Algebraic-Topology-to-Ventricular-Tachycardia) sits next to it. `python · tda · ecg`

**// agents & instruments**

- **[vega-rules](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/vega-rules)** — deterministic detection rules for a sales agent. The boring half of AI, which is the half that has to be right. `python`
- **[cody-cartridge](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/cody-cartridge)** — a Mac music player cosplaying as noir hardware. Local files only, works offline, the vibes are non-negotiable. `electron · react · ts`

**// proving grounds**

- **[USACOSolves](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/USACOSolves)** — the Platinum paper trail, in C++17. Bessie the cow has seen things. `c++`

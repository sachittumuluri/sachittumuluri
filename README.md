### hey, I'm Sachit

I build software that listens — to markets, to heartbeats, to music. Not a metaphor: those are literally the three things in this account.

Most of my time right now goes into **Vega Labs**, where I'm building AI agents for sales pipelines. My whole philosophy fits on a tape label: *judgment in the model, rules in code, receipts for everything.* Before that it was quant research with MAIF at UMass, and a stretch of proving you can catch heart arrhythmias with algebraic topology (you can — it works better than it has any right to).

Currently at UMass Amherst. Previously [@jpmorganchase](https://github.com/jpmorganchase) and [@PimBrands](https://github.com/PimBrands).

### the rack

- **[cody-cartridge](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/cody-cartridge)** — a Mac music player cosplaying as noir hardware. Local files only, works offline, the vibes are non-negotiable. `electron · react · ts`
- **[vega-rules](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/vega-rules)** — deterministic detection rules for a sales agent. The boring half of AI, which is the half that has to be right. `python`
- **[strattrust](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/strattrust)** — scorecards that make trading strategies show their work. If it can't survive a re-run, it wasn't alpha. `python · jupyter`
- **[maif-backtester](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/maif-backtester)** — MAIF Quant Lab's backtester and hosted research terminal. `python · jupyter`
- **[VentriCode](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/VentriCode)** — detecting ventricular tachycardia with topological data analysis. Donut math on heartbeats. `python · tda · ecg`
- **[USACOSolves](https://github.com/sachittumuluri/USACOSolves)** — years of USACO in C++17. Bessie the cow has seen things. `c++`
